Idea 01 / 21
Tier 1

01League rank + percentile, on every box.

Why

  • A 4.32 ERA looks identical to a 2.10 ERA today — same box, same color.
  • One thin colored bar + a "#38 of 142" caption tells you instantly whether a number is good.
  • Stops users from having to mentally compare against a league average they don't have memorized.

Idea 03 / 21
Tier 1

03Hot / Cold badges in lists.

Why

  • Badges in leader & roster rows show who is actually trending right now — not just season-to-date.
  • Threshold: last-15 OPS more than .080 above season → 🔥 HOT, .080 below → ❄ COLD.
  • Makes the Stats tab feel "live" without changing the data model.

Idea 05 / 21
Tier 1

05Qualified toggle on leaders.

Why

  • Today, a guy with 8 PA and 4 hits leads the team in AVG. Useless.
  • Qualified = PA ≥ 3.1 × team games (hitters), IP ≥ 1 × team games (pitchers).
  • Default ON. Toggle off when you want to see hot prospects with small samples.

Team Stats card
