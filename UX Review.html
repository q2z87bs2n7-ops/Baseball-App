09PolishAccessibility

Custom toggle widgets aren't real toggles.

<div onclick="togglePush()" role="checkbox"> — keyboard users can't activate them. No tabindex, no Space/Enter handler. The aria-label was added; the keyboard interaction never was. Same for invert, radio, master sound, sound prefs.

Fix<button role="switch" aria-checked="false">. Native focus, native space-to-activate, screen reader announces correctly.

16CounterbalanceDon't break these

What's already working — leave it alone.

Most of the structural craft in the app is real. Five things that should stay exactly as they are.

🧱

Section bar pattern

Eyebrow / context / toggles / overflow rhythm. Consistent across Pulse and Yesterday.

📱

iOS PWA chrome

Sticky 42 px header, bottom nav, safe-area-inset, separate iPad detection.

🎨

Theme-flash IIFE

Pre-paint hydration of CSS vars from localStorage. Most apps don't bother.

🃏

Card binder

Genuine product moment. Unexpected, delightful, on-brand.

🎯

Focus card + overlay

Highest-craft surface in the app. Pip dots, pitch sequence, switcher. Don't touch it.

17CloserPre-v5.0 sprint

If you only do five things before v5.0.

Ranked by perception-per-hour. The first two are five-line changes that raise the apparent quality bar more than another month of feature work.

Fix the title.

Stop advertising version numbers in browser tabs and bookmarks.

≈ 15 minutes

Next first-pitch, division mini, demo CTA. The default view shouldn't be a one-line shrug.

≈ 1 hour

Collapse the sheets.

≈ 45 minutes

Real toggles.
